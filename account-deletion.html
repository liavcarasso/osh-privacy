OSH — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: 2026-06-02

OSH is a shift-tracking app. This page explains how to delete your OSH account and all associated data. Account deletion is permanent and irreversible.

Option 1 — Delete inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open the OSH app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Settings tab.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm twice. Your account and data are erased immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, email contact@carasso.vip from the address associated with your account, with the subject Delete my OSH account. We will verify ownership and delete your account within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your Firebase Authentication credential (email/password login).
  • All shift records, job profiles, and earnings statistics.
  • Your friend relationships and group memberships.
  • Your push-notification token and your unique friend code.
  • Your uploaded profile photo (if any).

What is retained, and for how long

Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. We keep no separate backups of deleted user data beyond Google Firebase's own short technical retention window (typically up to 30 days in Google's backup systems), governed by Google's data-retention practices.
