## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve the App:

- **Providing the service** — displaying your shifts, earnings, and progress.
- **Calculations** — computing earnings based on the hourly rates and overtime rules you configure.
- **Social features** — showing your shift status and monthly totals to friends and group members you have explicitly connected with.
- **Push notifications** — alerting you to friend activity (friend requests, group updates) if you have enabled notifications.
- **Account security** — verifying your identity when you sign in.
- **Service improvement** — understanding how features are used so we can make them better. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs.

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App. By using Lumen, your data is also subject to their respective privacy policies.

### Firebase (Google LLC)

The App uses two Firebase services operated by Google LLC (1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA):

- **Firebase Authentication** — manages your email/password sign-in. Your email address is stored by Firebase Auth to identify your account. Firebase Auth is GDPR-compliant and offers a Data Processing Agreement.
- **Firebase Firestore** — our cloud database. Your shift records, job profiles, earnings aggregates, friend relationships, and group data are stored in Firestore. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access is governed by server-side Firestore Security Rules that ensure only you (and, where you have chosen, your friends and group members) can access your data.

Google's privacy policy is available at [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).  
Google's Firebase Data Processing and Security Terms are available at [https://firebase.google.com/terms/data-processing-terms](https://firebase.google.com/terms/data-processing-terms).

### Expo (Expo Inc.)

The App uses Expo's push notification infrastructure to route notifications from our Firebase project to your device (via Apple APNs on iOS or Google FCM on Android). Expo does not have access to the content of your data — only the device push token is passed through their system to deliver notifications.

Expo's privacy policy is available at [https://expo.dev/privacy](https://expo.dev/privacy).

### No Other Third Parties

We do not use:
- Advertising networks or ad SDKs
- Third-party analytics platforms (e.g., Mixpanel, Amplitude, Facebook Analytics)
- Data brokers or resale services
- Social media login (Facebook, Twitter, etc.)

## 7. Data Sharing Within the App

Lumen is designed with a privacy-first model. Sharing within the App is strictly opt-in:

- **Friends** — you must initiate or accept a friend request using a unique, randomly-generated friend code. Once connected, accepted friends can see your current shift status (on/off/paused) and your monthly earnings total. Your detailed shift records, hourly rates, and job profiles are **never** shared.
- **Groups** — you must join or create a group. Group members can see the same information as accepted friends for leaderboard purposes: your current shift status and monthly earnings total.
- **Privacy level** — you can set your account to "Private" in Settings, which hides your earnings from leaderboard displays within the App.
- **No public profiles** — your data is never visible to unauthenticated users or to Lumen users who are not your friends or group members.

---

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

If you delete your account (via **Settings → Delete Account** in the App), we permanently delete:
- Your Firebase Authentication credential
- All Firestore data: your shift records, job profiles, earnings stats, friend relationships, and group memberships
- Your push notification token
- Your unique friend code

Account deletion is irreversible. Deleted data cannot be recovered.

We do not retain backup copies of deleted user data beyond what Firebase's built-in infrastructure requires for its own operational purposes (typically a short technical retention window of up to 30 days in backup systems, which are governed by Google's own data retention practices).

---

## 9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption in transit** — all communication between the App and Firebase uses TLS encryption.
- **Encryption at rest** — Firebase Firestore encrypts data at rest by default.
- **Firestore Security Rules** — we use server-side Firestore Security Rules that enforce strict access controls. Only authenticated users can access data, and cross-user access is limited to the explicit sharing scenarios described in Section 7.
- **No plaintext passwords** — passwords are managed entirely by Firebase Authentication; we never see or store your password in plaintext.

Despite these measures, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.
